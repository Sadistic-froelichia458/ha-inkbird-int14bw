## Inkbird INT-14-BW

Local Bluetooth integration for the **Inkbird INT-14-BW / IBT-4XS** 4-probe
meat thermometer. Exposes all four probes and the battery level as native Home
Assistant sensors — no cloud, no MQTT, no custom firmware.

Works with a local Bluetooth adapter **or** an ESPHome Bluetooth proxy.

**Setup:** add the integration, then enter the thermometer's Bluetooth MAC
address (found in the Inkbird app under *Settings → Device Information*). That's
it.

See the [README](https://github.com/boris327/ha-inkbird-int14bw) for full
installation and troubleshooting instructions.
